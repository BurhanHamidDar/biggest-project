**How to Login:**
1.  Go to the Admin Panel URL.
2.  Enter your admin email and password.

**How to Add a New Student:**
1.  Navigate to **Students** > **Add Student**.
2.  Fill in the mandatory details (Name, Class, Section, Admission No).
3.  Click **Submit**. The system will auto-generate a password (default: `school123` or similar based on your config).

**How to Post a Notice:**
1.  Go to **Notices** > **Create Notice**.
2.  Enter a Title and Message.
3.  Select Target: "All" to notify everyone.
4.  Click **Publish**. A push notification is sent instantly.

**Emergency: Blocking the App:**
1.  Go to **Settings**.
2.  Toggle **"Block App Access"** to ON.
3.  Enter a reason (e.g., "Server Maintenance").
4.  Click **Save**. All mobile apps will now show this message.

## ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ« Teacher App Guide

**Getting Started:**
1.  Download the "Teacher App" from the link provided by Admin.
2.  Login with your credentials.

**Marking Attendance:**
1.  Tap **Attendance** on the dashboard.
2.  Select your Class and Section.
3.  You will see a list of students. Tap a student to change their status (Green = Present, Red = Absent).
4.  Click **Submit** to save as Draft.
5.  Click **Finalize** only when you are sure. *Note: You cannot edit after finalizing.*

**Entering Exam Marks:**
1.  Tap **Marks Entry**.
2.  Select the Exam and Subject.
3.  Enter marks for each student.
4.  Click **Save**.
